      <p>

        The Valsalva maneuver is the gold standard breathing technique for heavy lifting:
      </p>

      <h3>How to Do It:</h3>
      <ol>
        <li><strong>Inhale:</strong> Take a deep breath into your belly (not chest)</li>
        <li><strong>Brace:</strong> Tighten your core as if preparing to be punched</li>

      <h3>For Heavy Compound Lifts (Squat, Deadlift, Heavy Press)</h3>
      <ul>

        <li><strong>Hold:</strong> Throughout the entire rep</li>
        <li><strong>Exhale:</strong> At the top, after completing the rep</li>
      </ul>

      </p>

      <p>
