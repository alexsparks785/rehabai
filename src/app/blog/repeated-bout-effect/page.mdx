After the initial bout causes damage and soreness, subsequent bouts of the same exercise cause progressively less damage and soreness—even if the intensity increases.

      <p>

      </p>

      <p>

      </p>

      <p>
        The initial bout triggers an inflammatory response that "primes" the muscle's repair and protective mechanisms for future stress.
