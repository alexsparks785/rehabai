      <p>

      </p>

      <p>

      </p>

        Adding free weights dramatically expands your options:
      </p>

      </p>

        <li><strong>Selectorized (Bowflex, PowerBlock):</strong> More expensive, quick adjustment</li>
        <li><strong>Individual dumbbells:</strong> Most durable but require storage space</li>
      </ul>

      </p>
      <ul>
        <li>Weight capacity well above your body weight + dumbbells</li>
        <li>Stable legs that don't wobble</li>
        <li>Comfortable but firm padding</li>

      <p>

      </p>

      </p>

      <p>
        Plan for 8×10 feet or more, plus ceiling height (8+ feet ideal).
      </p>
